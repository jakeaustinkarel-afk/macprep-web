# MACPrep "What's New" update-post log

Tracks when the daily social drafter has used a **Product Update / What's New** post, so they stay occasional (~weekly at most), not daily. The drafter appends one line per update post:

`<YYYY-MM-DD> — update post covering: <short summary of items mentioned>`

Guardrail reminder: update posts cover **user-facing improvements only** — never security, payments/auth, infra, error-monitoring, or bug-fix internals. Prefer items already public on `updates.html`.
