# MACPrep for AA Programs
### The board prep that shows its work.

**MACPrep is an NCCAA board-review question bank where every question is research-driven and traceable
to a primary source — built to support your cohort's first-time CERT pass rate, and to teach, not just
test.**

---

## Why MACPrep is different
- **Research-driven, with a source on every question.** Every single item cites a primary research
  source students and faculty can click straight through to — no "trust us, it's evidence-based." A
  transparency badge sits on every question.
- **A full rationale on every question — for *every* answer choice.** Not just why the correct answer
  is right, but **why each incorrect option is wrong.** That's the difference between memorizing and
  understanding — and it's what makes MACPrep a teaching tool, not just a test bank.
- **Mapped to the official NCCAA blueprint** (2021 Job Analysis domain weightings) — your cohort
  practices in the exact proportions the exam tests.
- **Answer slots balanced (A=B=C=D=E)** to remove position bias — a rigor standard most banks skip.
