## ⭐ Your origin story — the main event (be ready to tell it well)
Arc: **pain → conviction → action → heart.** ~60–90 seconds, authentic, like talking to a friend.

- **The pain:** Back at South, board-prep materials were *insanely* expensive — on top of the student
  loans we were already drowning in. It always stung that the stuff meant to help you pass cost a fortune.
- **The conviction:** Prep should be rigorous but it shouldn't bankrupt students. And I never liked the
  subscription model — you never really own it, and one forgotten auto-renew and you're out money. I also
  wanted something I could fully trust: every question backed by a real research source, with a full
  rationale for every answer choice, right and wrong — the thing I wish I'd had.
- **The action:** I see students every day spending crazy money on prep, and it finally pushed me to
  build it. So MACPrep is NCCAA board review — research-driven, $50 one-time for life, no subscription —
  for SAAs on the cert exam and practicing CAAs on recert.
- **The heart:** Honestly I just wanted to build the thing I wish I'd had, and help people in my own
  field without gouging them.

_Patrick will connect with this fast — he built BagMask from the same "help the community" place._
