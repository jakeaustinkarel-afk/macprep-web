## How to use this
Two buckets: **competitors** (don't partner; monitor) and **complementary** ventures (real
partnership / co-marketing candidates). Pursue complementary only. Sequence partnership outreach
**after** the CAA Discord deal + program outreach are in motion — don't spread thin.

## ✅ Complementary — partnership / co-marketing candidates

  staffing firm + CAA job board. Strongest B2B fit — reaches practicing CAAs and new grads.
- **BagMask.com** — **Patrick Flaherty, CAA.** Anesthesia job board + quarterly CAA salary reports +
  scholarships. Jobs/salary reach + a natural scholarship tie-in.
- **Awakened Anesthetist** (podcast) — **Mary Jeanne Roberts, CAA.** First CAA-experience podcast +
  newsletter/community. High creator reach — guest spot or sponsorship.
- **CAA Lifestyle** — **Chabely Rodriguez, CAA** (~93K IG, CNBC-featured). Influencer reach to students
  and new grads.
- **AAAA — American Academy of Anesthesiologist Assistants** (anesthetist.org) — national society +
  annual conference. Top distribution target. _Caveat: runs its own CME — adjacent to a CME play._

  Good regional distribution.
