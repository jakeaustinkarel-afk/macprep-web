## Member-benefit terms (what you're offering)
- **20% off**, exclusive to the academy's members, via a **unique per-academy code** (e.g. `FAAA20`) —
  so redemptions are trackable and the academy can see the benefit getting used. (Same style as the
  cohort/classmate codes, just one per organization.)
- Applies to MACPrep Premium ($50 one-time lifetime) → **~$40 lifetime** for members. No subscription.
- MACPrep provides: the code + a co-branded "member benefit" graphic (academy logo + MACPrep) they can post.
- Academy provides: promotion to members (email, socials, member portal / benefits page).
- Reciprocal: MACPrep cross-promotes the academy (a "join your state academy" nudge) to relevant users.
- Optional upgrade: for a big student push, offer the academy free codes for a student event/scholarship.

## Co-promo graphic (offer to make it)
Mirror the FAAA×Vigilant "NEW member benefit" style but in MACPrep's brand: academy logo + MACPrep
pulse mark, "New member benefit · 20% off NCCAA board prep · $50 lifetime → $40 for members." Keep it
on-brand (green/ink), never comparative.
