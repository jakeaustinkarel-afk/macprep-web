Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how MACPrep uses cookies and similar browser storage technologies. We keep this minimal: we use only what is needed to run the Service and do not use third-party advertising or cross-site tracking cookies.

What we use

TechnologyPurposeType
Local storage (macprep_token)Keeps you signed in by storing your session token in your browser.Strictly necessary
Local storage (preferences)Remembers small UI preferences such as your premium status flag.Functional
Supabase auth storageUsed by our authentication provider to manage your secure session.Strictly necessary

What we do not use

  • No advertising or marketing cookies.
  • No third-party cross-site tracking or analytics profiling.
  • No selling of any browsing data.

Managing storage

Because we rely on strictly necessary session storage, signing out or clearing your browser's site data for macprep.org will remove these items. Note that clearing them will sign you out.

Changes

We may update this policy; the "last updated" date reflects the latest version.
