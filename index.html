📊 Section 1: Psychometrics & Adaptive Tracking

Q: How does the adaptive performance engine handle accuracy updates?

MACPrep monitors selections dynamically using categorical weight rules. Your total answers cleared, total correct inputs, and individual specialty performance metrics are computed in real time. These metrics are instantly committed to cloud-synchronized data clusters upon option selection to provide immediate visual progression tracking.

Q: Are these questions aligned with official certification blueprints?

Yes. Every question is rigorously designed to fulfill the content parameters established by national certifying boards. This includes comprehensive coverage of the 8 core clinical modalities required for both initial certification and ongoing Demonstration of Qualifications (CDQ) recertification pathways.

💳 Section 2: Account Licensing & Access Tiers

Q: What restrictions are placed on non-premium trial sessions?

Free registration sessions are restricted to a 10% un-gated capacity across all active modules. Upgrading to a premium license instantly removes this trial ceiling, unlocking unrestricted navigation through the entire 1,000-question library and enabling custom exam block configurations.

Q: Can I access my profile and scoreboard across multiple devices?

Yes. Because your metrics are tethered directly to your database profile via authenticated cloud streams, logging into any desktop web instance safely synchronizes your entire state tracking profile, avatar, and specialty analytics data points.
