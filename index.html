🛡️ Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2026. Data Governance, Compliance, and Security Statement.

1. Data Collection & Processing Scope

MACPrep collects minimal professional identifier data—specifically account email addresses and profile metrics—solely to maintain persistent session metrics. No personal medical information or telemetry data from private surgical practices is captured or parsed.

2. Encryption & Storage Safeguards

Session tokens, payment status flags, and performance histories are isolated behind layered encryption protocols. Local client parameters track securely inside storage clusters with cryptographic sign-off rules enforced by Supabase security modules.

3. Institutional Third-Party Sharing Rules

Individual practitioner records, miss patterns, and calculation scores are confidential. Personal testing vectors are never shared, sold, or distributed to licensing agencies, hospital networks, or third-party academic channels.

4. Compliance & User Data Rights

In alignment with professional evaluation platform data safety standards, practitioners retain full deletion rights. Clicking "Sign Out" or requesting an administrative clear completely purges cached accounts from our tables within 24 hours.
