    PHARMACOLOGY · LOCAL ANESTHETICS

    Minutes after a large-volume regional block, a patient reports perioral numbness, tinnitus, and a metallic taste. What is the most appropriate next step?

    A Continue the block and reassess in 5 minutes
    B Stop the injection and prepare 20% lipid emulsion
    ✓Correct. Perioral numbness, tinnitus, and a metallic taste are early signs of local anesthetic systemic toxicity — stop the injection and start 20% lipid emulsion.
    C Administer a beta-blocker for the tinnitus
    D Increase the local anesthetic dose
    Cited source · ASRA Practice Advisory on LAST (2018)
